The Problem

The non-profit organization, Much Love Animal Rescue website provides an overwhelming amount of information and yet users struggle to find information about how to care for a rescue pet and donâ€™t understand the adoption process.

The Solution

Re-create the website so calls to action are more accessible and relevant information/processes are clearly displayed.

Research Strategy

Conducted online survey and received 10 responses

Conducted 5 one-on-one interviews to help determine what motivates individuals to adopt or foster pets, volunteer at animal shelters or donate to animal shelters.

Utilized the SWOT method to complete 3 competitor analysis of other animal rescue organizations.

Proto Persona and User Insight

Jackie Lopez often feels drained after her stressful job as a school counselor. She loves animals and feels they help her destress. She wants to adopt a pet but she's not sure she's ready for the commitment and doesn't feel she's knows how to properly care for an animal.

She needs to find detailed, easy to understand information on how to adopt and care for animals so she can adopt a pet and de-stress in her free time.

Project Overview

Overall, during the second round of usability testing, users indicated the rebranding and redesign of the website increased accessibility of the information and ease of use. There were additional process flows pending completion such as the volunteer portal and connecting the foster process flow with adoption.
